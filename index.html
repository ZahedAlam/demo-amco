About AMCO

AMCO Apparel Manufacturing Company is the leading Apparel manufacturing company from United Arab Emirates, near the dynamic city of Dubai. For the past 5 decades we have been the manufacturing partners for known apparel brands from USA and EUROPE. We are serving our customers with a complete apparel manufacturing solution from sourcing of raw materials to finished goods. Having a comprehensive in-house set up, best labor practices, ISO, WRAP, SEDEX, GRS and OCS certified production facility, in addition we are able to offer shorter lead time than Asia due to our geographically central location and produce a high quality product in terms of all respects.

Outer Wear

We manufacture all styles in outerwear which include fleece jackets, wind breakers, sports jacket/trousers, bomber jackets, puffer vest, quilted jackets, anorak, varsity jackets, heavy duty parka jackets, lightwear and mid weight jackets.
